P2Customization concept

If cards are user-customizable, here's the mental model. Each user picks their template; their card renders in that template for everyone viewing the people pane. Confirm the model, or shape it differently.

Card template

How your profile appears in everyone's people pane.

Each of the 10 V5 people-pane prototypes can hardcode a default template, but at the model level we'd store user.card_template on the profile. Three concrete options for how this manifests across the 10 prototypes:

  • P2.uniform — Each prototype enforces a single template (10 = 10 styles). User can't customize within a prototype, but moving between prototypes changes the look.
  • P2.mixed — Each prototype renders a directory where each member's card uses their own chosen template. Cards in one feed are visually heterogeneous.
  • P2.hybrid — Prototype has a default skin, but logged-in user can override their own card. 80% homogeneous, 20% individual expression.
