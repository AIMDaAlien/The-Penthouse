33 icon directions. The PENT 2x2 grid family leads, non-grid PENT follows, PH interlock and experimental round out. Each tile shows the hero at 168 px, plus a scale row at PWA-install (192), Apple touch (60), and favicon (16). Tap a tile to enlarge.

Wordmark stays separate. None of these replace it. They are compressed marks meant to survive at 16 px where the wordmark's hairline serifs would not. Generated from the same SVG source as the live prototype at /prototypes/logo.
