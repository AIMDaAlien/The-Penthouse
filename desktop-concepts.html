The Penthouse — Desktop Architecture

Scaling luxury chat to desktop means enforcing strict reading widths and leveraging generous whitespace. Below are three distinct architectural approaches for desktop scaling. The UI uses all settled styles: Wider Avatar Gap, Glassy Bubbles (Spatial Gap only), Bright Periwinkle, Faded Double Track Dividers, Deep Depth of Field transitions, and the Floating Inner Banner Profile Card.

Note: You can use the bottom-right corner of the containers to resize them horizontally and test fluidity.

Option A: The Editorial Split (Master-Detail)

The standard elevated. A fixed chat list pane on the left, and a fluid reading pane on the right. The chat thread is strictly constrained to 720px in the center, wrapped in luxurious whitespace on wide monitors.
