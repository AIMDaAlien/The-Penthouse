# Welcome to The Penthouse (Alpha)

The Penthouse is a private, invite-only messaging app built for small groups of people who actually know each other. You were invited because the owner vouches for you and values your presence here.

This is an early alpha release. It’s a bit rough around the edges, but it’s a space where we can talk without the noise of the public web.

---

### 1. How to Install
The Penthouse is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You don't need an app store; you just add it to your home screen.

**On iPhone (Safari):**
1.  Open `https://penthouse.blog` in Safari.
2.  Tap the **Share** icon (the square with an upward arrow).
3.  Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4.  Confirm the name and tap **Add**.

**On Android (Chrome):**
1.  Open `https://penthouse.blog` in Chrome.
2.  Tap the **three-dot menu** in the top right.
3.  Tap **Add to Home Screen** (may appear as 'Install app' on newer Chrome).
4.  Confirm the installation.

Once installed, The Penthouse will live on your home screen and open in its own fullscreen window, just like any other app.

---

### 2. Getting Started
*   **Register:** When you first enter, you'll need to create an account with a username and password.
*   **Message:** Start a direct message with anyone else who has registered.
*   **Sync:** If you have the app open, messages appear in real-time. If you’re away, they’ll be waiting for you the next time you open the app.

---

### 3. A Few Rough Edges
Since we’re in alpha, please keep these limitations in mind:
*   **No Push Notifications:** You won’t get a buzz in your pocket when a message arrives yet. You'll need to open the app to check for new activity.
*   **Text Only:** For now, we're sticking to words. Image and file sharing will come later.
*   **If it looks stuck:** If the interface seems broken or doesn't update, try a "hard reload." You can pull down to refresh, or simply close and reopen the app.
